My services

(*) Design, optimisation and implementation of agricultural projects, specifically sustainable livestock farming

(*) Valorisation of available resources at the level of the agricultural community via:

- the use of innovative and adapted technologies

- sustainable and efficient technology transfer systems

(*) Support of development organizations, communities, associations, agricultural businesses or individual farmers on:

- the design and optimisation of breeding strategies

- Adapted management strategies

- breeding objectives and genetic improvement programs

- monitoring and evaluation of the use of genetic diversity and climate change

I have a particular interest in the integration of interdisciplinary information (genetics + biotechnologies + economy + environment + technology transfer)

(*) Carrying out actions aimed to:

- genetic and genomic characterization of farm animals

- the transition towards organic and agroecological systems

- the digital transition favoring the on-farm data digitilisation and its valuation.

- the formulation of projects integrating the conservation and characterization of biodiversity with the appropriate routes of use.

(*) Support to redesign and define robust strategies within the framework of:

- expected climate change scenarios

- the new challenges of agriculture in terms of precision, competitiveness and sustainability respecting societal aspects, animal welfare while preserving the environment.
