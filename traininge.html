Training activities carried out


I have participated in the training of future engineers and technicians in the field of animal production sciences and data modeling. I have taught several promotions on applied biometrics, computing technics and statistics courses at ESAM (Tunisia). I trained MSc studens on the automation of information systems,management and exploration of breeding data (ISA Chott Meriem, Tunisia). As part of development projects on breeding and management of animal resources, I participated in the training of consulting engineers for the valuation of the results of performance monitoring, herd management and genetic selection.


I also temporarely assisted in the teaching and supervision of engineers, master's students and PhD students in the field of quantitative genetics, animal improvement and digital modeling (ULiège-GxABT, Belgium).


As part of multi-partner research projects (eg OPTIMIR, GplusE), I was active as convener in monitoring,evaluation and communication with partners (milk recording organisations, Data Scientists).
